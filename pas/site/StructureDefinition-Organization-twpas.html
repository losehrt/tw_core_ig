Resource Profile: 醫事機構-Organization TWPAS

Official URL: https://nhicore.nhi.gov.tw/pas/StructureDefinition/Organization-twpas Version: 1.0.6
Active as of 2025-07-30 Computable Name: OrganizationTWPAS

此醫事機構-Organization TWPAS Profile說明本IG如何進一步定義臺灣核心-機構(TW Core Organization) Profile以呈現癌藥事前審查之醫事機構基本資料

Usages:

  • Use this Profile: 事前審查-Bundle TWPAS
  • Refer to this Profile: 事前審查-Claim TWPAS
  • Examples for this Profile: 臺北市立聯合醫院
  • CapabilityStatements using this Profile: 臺灣癌藥事前審查-用戶端(TWPAS Client) and 臺灣癌藥事前審查-伺服端(TWPAS Server)

You can also check for usages in the FHIR IG Statistics
