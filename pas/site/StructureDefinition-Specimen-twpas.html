Resource Profile: 基因檢測檢體-Specimen TWPAS

Official URL: https://nhicore.nhi.gov.tw/pas/StructureDefinition/Specimen-twpas Version: 1.0.6
Active as of 2025-07-30 Computable Name: SpecimenTWPAS

此基因檢測檢體-Specimen TWPAS Profile說明本IG如何進一步定義臺灣核心-檢體(TW Core Specimen) Profile以呈現癌藥事前審查中基因資訊之檢測檢體相關內容

Usages:

  • Use this Profile: 事前審查-Bundle TWPAS
  • Refer to this Profile: 基因資訊-Observation Diagnostic TWPAS
  • Examples for this Profile: Specimen/spe-min
  • CapabilityStatements using this Profile: 臺灣癌藥事前審查-用戶端(TWPAS Client) and 臺灣癌藥事前審查-伺服端(TWPAS Server)

You can also check for usages in the FHIR IG Statistics
